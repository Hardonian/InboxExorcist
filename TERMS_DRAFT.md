# Terms Draft

InboxExorcist is provided to help users identify and quiet promotional Gmail senders.

The app does not guarantee that every sender can be unsubscribed, filtered, or identified. Unsubscribe success is reported only when the standards-based attempt is confirmed or attempted by the upstream mechanism. Filtering is reported separately.

InboxExorcist never deletes emails by default. Users remain responsible for reviewing proposed actions before confirming quieting.

This draft is not legal advice and must be reviewed before launch.
